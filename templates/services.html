Our Services

Comprehensive eye care services tailored to your individual needs

Comprehensive Eye Care

At Dr. Richa's Eye Clinic, we offer a wide range of eye care services using the latest technologies and treatment methods. Our goal is to provide personalized care that addresses your specific vision needs and concerns.

Whether you need a routine eye examination, treatment for an eye condition, or surgical intervention, our team of experienced professionals is here to help you achieve optimal eye health and vision.

Comprehensive Eye Examination

Our thorough eye examinations assess your vision and eye health, including:

  • Visual acuity testing
  • Refraction assessment
  • Eye pressure measurement
  • Anterior and posterior segment evaluation
  • Digital retinal imaging

Recommended annually for most patients

Price: ₹500 - ₹1,500

Cataract Surgery

Advanced cataract removal procedures with premium lens options:

  • Phacoemulsification
  • Monofocal lens implants
  • Multifocal lens implants
  • Toric lens for astigmatism
  • Same-day surgery with minimal recovery time

Includes pre-operative evaluation and post-operative care

Price: ₹25,000 - ₹80,000 (depends on lens type)

Glaucoma Management

Comprehensive glaucoma care services including:

  • Intraocular pressure monitoring
  • Visual field testing
  • Optical coherence tomography (OCT)
  • Medical management
  • Selective laser trabeculoplasty
  • Minimally invasive glaucoma surgery

Regular follow-ups required for monitoring

Price: ₹700 - ₹2,500 (consultation)
₹15,000 - ₹40,000 (procedures)

Pediatric Eye Care

Specialized eye care services for children:

  • Age-appropriate vision testing
  • Amblyopia (lazy eye) treatment
  • Strabismus evaluation and management
  • Pediatric eye examinations
  • Child-friendly environment and approach

First examination recommended at age 3 or earlier if concerns

Price: ₹700 - ₹1,500

Diabetic Eye Care

Specialized care for patients with diabetes:

  • Diabetic retinopathy screening
  • Digital retinal imaging
  • Fundus fluorescein angiography
  • Laser photocoagulation
  • Intravitreal injections

Annual examinations recommended for all diabetic patients

Price: ₹800 - ₹2,000 (screening)
₹8,000 - ₹15,000 (procedures)

Refractive Error Correction

Solutions for vision correction:

  • Specialty contact lenses
  • Refractive surgery consultation
  • LASIK and PRK referrals

Customized solutions based on your lifestyle and preferences

Price: ₹600 - ₹2,000 (consultation/fitting)
